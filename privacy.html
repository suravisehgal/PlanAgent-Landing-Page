Privacy & security

Privacy Policy

We build PlanAgent to protect your personal data, support secure student verification, and give you control over your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, username, subscription preferences.
  • Device Data: Device type, browser information, locale, and performance metrics.
  • Usage Analytics: Feature usage, session duration, and productivity insights.
  • Support Requests: Messages, verification documents, and service ticket details.

Why We Collect Data

  • Service Improvements: Make planning faster, smarter, and more reliable.
  • Personalization: Tailor recommendations, workflows, and student offers.
  • Security: Detect abuse and keep your account safe.
  • Product Optimization: Improve features, onboarding, and support experiences.

Student Verification

Student verification is handled securely to confirm eligibility for discounted plans. Documents are encrypted, stored briefly for validation, and deleted on request.

We never share verification details with third parties without consent.

User Rights

  • Access: Request the personal data we hold about you.
  • Edit: Update account information and preferences anytime.
  • Export: Download your planning data and analytics reports.
  • Delete: Remove your account and associated records with one request.

Security Measures

  • Encryption: 256-bit encryption protects data at rest and in transit.
  • Monitoring: Continuous security monitoring and access auditing.
  • Data Protection: Role-based access and secure backup protocols.
